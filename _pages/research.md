## Research Overview

My research lies at the intersection of **computer vision**, **multimodal learning**, and **robust AI systems**. I focus on developing scalable and reliable machine learning solutions that can be deployed in real-world settings, particularly in healthcare and sustainability.

### Core Research Areas
**Vision Foundation Models for Brain MRI**

I am currently working on **vision foundation models** for brain MRI analysis. These models focus on learning rich visual representations from MRI scans to support diagnostic reasoning for neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer’s and dementia, when integrated with clinical context.

**Multimodal Foundation Models**  
Developing models that integrate multiple data modalities (e.g., medical images + clinical text).
- **Brain-FM**: A multimodal foundation model for visual question answering in brain health diagnostics, with applications in Alzheimer’s and dementia analysis (Top 10 Finalist, AI Health World Summit 2025).
- Development of clinician-validated datasets (e.g., VGMED) and analysis of visual grounding in medical multimodal LLMs (**ICLR 2026**).

**Self-Supervised Learning for Industrial Applications**
- **Hypersense AI**: Multispectral self-supervised framework for automated plastic waste resin classification.  
  → Led to Singapore Provisional Patent (2024) and PCT International Application (2025).
