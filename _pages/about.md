## AI Researcher | Computer Vision & Multimodal Learning

PhD in Computer Science (Computer Vision) from **Monash University**.

I develop robust and scalable AI solutions with a strong focus on **multimodal foundation models**, **self-supervised learning**, and **out-of-distribution generalization**. My work spans healthcare, sustainability, and scientific applications.

### Selected Highlights
- **Brain-FM**: Developed a **multimodal foundation model** for visual question answering in brain health, supporting Alzheimer’s and dementia-related analysis. Selected as a **Top 10 Finalist** at the **AI Health World Summit 2025**.
- **Hypersense AI**: Created a multispectral self-supervised learning framework for automated plastic waste sorting, leading to a **Singapore provisional patent (2024)** and **PCT international application (2025)**.
- Publications in top venues: **ICLR 2026**, **NeurIPS 2024**, **IEEE ICIP 2024**, etc.
